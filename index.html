Our Story

Est. 2026

Born From Nature, Built for Tomorrow

COCOAL was born from a simple observation: millions of coconut shells go to waste every year while traditional charcoal production devastates forests. We saw an opportunity to transform agricultural waste into a premium grilling product that's better for both your food and the planet.

Our team of BSBA Marketing students from NU Lipa developed a proprietary carbonization process that locks in natural smoke flavor while eliminating harmful chemicals found in conventional briquettes.

Eco-Certified. 100% Sustainable Material

Our briquettes are produced using renewable biomass and natural binders, ensuring zero harmful additives and minimal environmental impact.

Our Vision

A world where cooking fuel comes from renewable waste streams—not from cutting down forests. A circular economy where every coconut shell has purpose.

Our Mission

To provide high-performance, eco-friendly briquettes that reduce deforestation, support local communities, and enhance grilling quality.

Expert Cooking Tips

  • Preheat Properly: Allow the briquettes to ash over for 10–15 minutes before cooking for even heat.
  • Maintain Airflow: Ensure your grill's vents are open. COCOAL thrives on steady oxygen supply.
  • Avoid Overcrowding: Leave space between the briquettes and the food to prevent flare-ups and ensure an even cook.
